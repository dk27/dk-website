SpaceX Data Exploration (Part 2)

June 7th, 2020

May 30th 2020 was a historic day in the history of space explorationg. For the first time in 9 years, a manned spaceX spacecraft with two NASA astrounats on board successfully launched from Cape Canaveral at 3:22 pm. The first attempt was supposed to be on May 27th, but unfortunately the weather didn't cooperate. As we discovered in my previous post , SpaceX would like to enable people to live on other planet, and I think this launch made it one step closer to accomplishing just that.

In this post I'd like to look at launches API which contains information about every SpaceX launches, status of a launch, customers, etc. I'd also like to explore rockets endpoint.
