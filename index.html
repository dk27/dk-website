About Me

I'm a Data Scientist who is passionate about data science and analytics. My background is in Economics and Statistics, and I have been working as a Data Scientist for a mid-sized insurance company in the US for over six years. I truly enjoy doing what I'm doing and working on a variety of different data science whether it's a data prepping, machine learning or development project. In the last six years I have learned a lot about insurance, data science and programming.

I'd like to share tutorials, links to articles and other data science related things on my blog.

Besides data science, I love to spend my free time with my cat, CrossFit and travelling the world.
